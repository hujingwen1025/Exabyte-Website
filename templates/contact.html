Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Exabyte

Is Exabyte free?

Yes! Our core tools and resources are completely free. We're supported by our community and partners who believe in our mission.

Can I use Exabyte commercially?

Absolutely. Our open source projects are licensed for commercial use. Check individual project licenses for details.

How do I report a security issue?

Please email security@exabyte.org.cn with details. We take security seriously and will respond promptly.

Do you accept donations?

Yes! Donations help us continue our work. Visit our GitHub for sponsorship options and donation methods.

Where can I find documentation?

All documentation is available on GitHub and our website. If you can't find what you need, reach out to us.

How can I stay updated?

Follow us on social media, join our Discord community, or subscribe to our newsletter for updates.
